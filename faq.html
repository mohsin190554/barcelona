Booking
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle and time slot. For peak periods like public holidays, summer, or weekends, booking 48–72 hours ahead is advisable. For weddings and events, book as early as possible. That said, last-minute bookings are accepted based on availability — just contact us directly via WhatsApp.
Simply fill in the booking form on our homepage. Enter your pickup location, destination, date, time, number of passengers, and choose your vehicle. Then pay securely via PayPal and you'll receive instant confirmation. The whole process takes under 3 minutes.
Absolutely. Just enter the passenger's name and contact details in the booking form. We'll communicate directly with the passenger on the day to confirm pickup details and send the driver's information.
Yes. You can book one-way, a return on the same route, or two separate journeys at different times. Select the "Return" tab on the booking form. Return bookings often come with a small discount — feel free to ask us when booking.
Yes — changes to date, time, destination, or vehicle can be made at any time subject to availability. Simply contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking reference and the changes you need. We'll confirm the update as quickly as possible.
Payment
Payment is made securely at the time of booking via PayPal. You do not need a PayPal account — you can pay directly with any Visa, Mastercard, or American Express card. Your booking is confirmed instantly once payment is completed.
Yes, completely. PayPal uses industry-leading encryption and fraud detection. Your card details are never stored on our servers — all payment processing is handled entirely by PayPal's secure infrastructure.
None whatsoever. The price you see when booking is the price you pay. There are no fuel surcharges, no toll additions, no late-night fees. Meet & Greet service, child seats, and booster seats are all included at no extra cost.
Yes. PayPal automatically sends a payment receipt to your email. If you need a formal invoice for business expenses, contact us after your booking and we'll issue one with all required details.
If you cancel within our free cancellation window (24 hours before pickup), a full refund will be processed back to your original payment method within 3–5 business days. Refunds are handled through PayPal.
Vehicles & Comfort
We operate four premium vehicles: a Mercedes V-Class (up to 7 passengers), Dodge Durango SUV (up to 6), Lexus E400 executive sedan (up to 4), and a Luxury Sedan (up to 3). Each is maintained to the highest standard. See our Fleet page for full details.
Yes, child seats and booster seats are available at absolutely no extra charge. Simply select the option when filling in the booking form. Seats are installed and safety-checked before your pickup.
The Mercedes V-Class and Dodge Durango both accommodate 6–7 large suitcases with ease. The Lexus and Luxury Sedan fit 3 large bags comfortably. If you have oversized or unusual luggage, let us know in the special requests field and we'll make sure the right vehicle is assigned.
All vehicles have full climate control. In summer we keep it cool, in winter it's warm — and the temperature can be adjusted to your preference throughout the journey.
Wi-Fi is available in the Lexus E400 and Luxury Sedan as standard. It can be requested in other vehicles — just mention it in your special requests when booking and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Airport Transfers
Yes — always. We monitor your flight in real time using live flight tracking. If your flight is delayed, your driver automatically adjusts their arrival time. You never need to call us to update — we already know.
We include 60 minutes of free waiting time for all airport arrivals after your flight lands. This gives you time to clear immigration, collect baggage, and exit the terminal without any rush. Beyond 60 minutes, a small waiting fee may apply — but this is rarely needed.
Your driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall with a name board showing your name. For Barcelona El Prat airport, we meet at the main arrivals exit of your terminal. If you have any difficulty locating your driver, the contact number will be in your confirmation email.
Yes, Meet & Greet is included at no extra charge. Your driver enters the arrivals hall and waits with a personalised name board. This is the default for all airport pickups — no need to select it separately.
Of course. For departures, simply provide your flight number and terminal in the booking form and we'll drop you directly at the correct entrance with plenty of time to check in.
Policies
We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. To cancel, contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking reference.
We understand that plans change unexpectedly. Contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel at short notice. We assess each situation individually and will always try to find a fair resolution — especially in cases of genuine emergencies.
No — all our vehicles are strictly non-smoking. This includes e-cigarettes and vapes. We appreciate your cooperation in keeping our fleet fresh and comfortable for every passenger.
Pets are welcome with advance notice. Please mention this in the special requests field when booking so we can prepare the appropriate vehicle and any necessary protection for the seats.
Contact us as soon as possible via phone or WhatsApp. Our drivers check vehicles after every journey, and if an item is found it will be secured safely. We'll arrange return of your belongings at the earliest opportunity.
