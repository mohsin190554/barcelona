Your privacy matters to us. EliteRide Barcelona is committed to protecting your personal data and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Spanish data protection laws.

1. Introduction

EliteRide Barcelona ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller responsible for your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

Our registered business operates in Barcelona, Spain. For any data-related enquiries, contact us at info@eliteride.es.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data when you interact with our services:

Data TypeExamplesSource
Identity DataFull nameBooking form
Contact DataPhone number, email addressBooking / contact form
Journey DataPickup/drop-off location, date, time, flight numberBooking form

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as race, religion, health information, or biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfil your booking — confirming your journey, coordinating your driver, and sending confirmation details
  • To communicate with you — responding to enquiries, sending booking updates, and contacting you about your journey
  • To process payment — via PayPal's secure payment processing system
  • To comply with legal obligations — maintaining records as required by Spanish law
  • To improve our service — analysing booking patterns and service performance (using anonymised data only)

We do not use your personal data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we do not sell your data to any third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing is necessary to fulfil the transfer service you have booked
  • Legal obligation — processing is required to comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Legitimate interests — processing for fraud prevention, security, and service improvement
  • Consent — where you have explicitly consented (e.g. to receive marketing communications)

5. Data Sharing

We share your personal data only with the following parties and only to the extent necessary:

  • Our drivers — your name, phone number, pickup location, and journey details are shared with your assigned driver to fulfil your booking
  • PayPal — payment data is processed by PayPal in accordance with their own privacy policy
  • Supabase — our booking data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure (based in the EU)
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or court order

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

  • Booking records: 5 years (required for Spanish tax and commercial law compliance)
  • Contact form messages: 12 months from the date of receipt
  • Payment records: 5 years (retained by PayPal in accordance with their policies)

After the applicable retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Right to restriction — request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@eliteride.es. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD) at www.aepd.es.

8. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data storage via Supabase
  • Secure HTTPS connections on our website
  • Access controls limiting who can view booking data
  • Payment processing entirely via PayPal — we never see or store your card details

9. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for the website to function correctly. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. For full details on cookies used, please see our Cookie Policy.

10. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • PayPal — payment processing (paypal.com/privacy)
  • Supabase — database and backend (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google Fonts — typography (policies.google.com/privacy)
