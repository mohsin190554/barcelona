8. Delays & Waiting Time

Airport Pickups

For airport arrivals, we include 60 minutes of complimentary waiting time calculated from the actual landing time of your flight. We track all flights in real time — no need to contact us if your flight is delayed.

Non-Airport Pickups

For all other pickups, we allow 15 minutes of complimentary waiting time from the agreed pickup time. Waiting beyond this may incur an additional charge.

Driver Delays

If your driver is delayed due to circumstances beyond our control, we will notify you as soon as possible and adjust accordingly at no extra charge.
