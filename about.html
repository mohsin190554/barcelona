Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Passion

Royal taxi Services was founded with a single belief — that every journey deserves to be exceptional. We grew tired of seeing travellers arrive stressed, squeezed into uncomfortable vehicles, or left waiting with no communication.

So we built something different. A private transfer service that puts comfort, punctuality, and genuine care at the centre of every single ride. From the moment you book to the moment you arrive, you are our priority.

Based in Barcelona and operating across Spain and beyond, we serve business executives, families, couples, and everyone in between — all with the same uncompromising level of service.

"We don't just drive people from A to B. We create the kind of arrival that sets the tone for an entire trip."
— Founder, Royal taxi Services

How We Got Here

2019
The Beginning
Royal taxi Services was founded with a single vehicle and a commitment to providing Barcelona's best private transfer experience. First 50 clients served in the opening month.

Added the Mercedes V-Class and Dodge Durango to the fleet, allowing us to serve larger groups and corporate clients. Airport transfer partnerships established.
